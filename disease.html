About Disease Types

Diseases are categorized into several types: Viral, Metabolic, Cardiovascular, and Parasitic. Each type has unique causes and affects the body differently.

Viral Diseases

Diseases caused by viruses. Spread via droplets, surfaces, or contact.

Influenza (Flu)

Causes: Influenza virus transmitted via droplets

COVID-19

Causes: SARS-CoV-2 virus transmitted via droplets or surfaces

Metabolic Disorders

Disorders that affect metabolism. Example: Diabetes.

Diabetes

Causes: Insufficient insulin production or insulin resistance

Obesity

Causes: Poor diet, lack of exercise, genetics

Cardiovascular Conditions

Diseases of the heart and blood vessels, such as hypertension and heart disease.

Hypertension

Causes: High blood pressure due to genetics, diet, or lifestyle

Parasitic Infections

Diseases caused by parasites, often spread via insects or contaminated food/water.

Malaria

Causes: Plasmodium parasite transmitted by mosquito bites

Other Diseases

Additional examples of diseases for reference.

Chickenpox

Causes: Varicella-zoster virus
