About Diseases

Diseases are conditions that negatively affect the structure or function of your body. They can be caused by viruses, bacteria, parasites, or lifestyle factors. Staying hydrated, exercising regularly, and getting enough sleep can help prevent many common diseases and keep your body healthy.
